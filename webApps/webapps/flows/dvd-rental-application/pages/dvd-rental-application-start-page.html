About us

The DVD Shop Archives is constantly preserving and digitising its physical formats in order to safeguard material that is physically deteriorating for the future. In addition, our aspiration is to create a largely file based environment where we no longer have to move physical media around theTV Vault and instead can continue to preserve original master material in secure vaults. This DVD Shop now operates in a fully digital environment with both Television and Radio material created as digital files and, to support this, the TV Vault has put in place digital repositories for storage of this material, and to enable public access. We are always adding classic dvds to our shop check back for updates.

Returns Policy


We here at The DVD Shop want you to be happy with your purchase. We strive to create the very best products available.

1. Due to the growth of dvd recordable technology, The DVD Shop regrettably does not offer refunds on products already shipped, as this would be open to abuse.

2. In the event of a problem with your purchase, you should email us outlining the nature of the problem. If you experience difficulty with a disc, you may be required to return the original disc so that a replacement can be sent out to you. Shipping and Handling charges are not refundable except in the instance of an error on the part of The DVD Shop.

3. The DVD Shop respectfully reminds all customers that it is their responsibility to check that their DVD Player is suitable for playing Pal Dvd-r products before they order. No refunds will be given for incompatibility issues.
